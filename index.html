Le Choux

Welcome to Le Choux, a French patisserie in London serving choux buns filled with fresh cream and poured with your choice of chocolate and salted caramel sauce. as a series of larger cream filled choux.

Le Choux is a representation of our passion for artisan methods and great ingredients. All our choux buns are made with free range eggs and organic flour, then hand piped and filled with fresh locally sourced cream.

We use great chocolate and our patisseries are naturally free from preservatives and artificial colourings.

Le Choux also offers chouquettes, a mini choux bun sprinkled with pearl sugar as well
